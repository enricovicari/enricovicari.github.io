Vine quiz

Making quizzes on SparkAR isn't easy but it's so rewarding. I used viral Vines for this quiz. Made the UI on Figma, then imported the assets on SparkAR and added a "score" logic with Javascript. The filter got over 200K impressions but got disabled after about 4 months because it "wasn't respecting community guidelines".
